About Zyron

Zexon Development is a private organization, branded as Zexon, that has created projects like Zyron, Zexon Script, and other Roblox Projects. Zexon and Zyron do not collect any data whatsoever. It only connects to your selected executor and stores data on your device.

Zyron is a custom UI layer for Roblox executors. Zyron is not an executor itself; it connects to Hydrogen or MacSploit so you can execute scripts with a cleaner interface.
